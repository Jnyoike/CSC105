Introduction

Welcome to the Smith College Outing Club website! Browse photos, upcoming events, and club history to get to know what makes the Outing Club unique here at Smith.

The Smith College Outing Club finds ways to bring Smith students together through enjoying the great outdoors.

From coordinating hikes with other organizations to hosting our annual Concinnity Festival.

The Outing Club prides itself on creating a safe, diverse, and fun community full of people who enjoy spending time outside.

If you have any questions about the club, feel free to send us an email or check out our social media pages under Contact Us
