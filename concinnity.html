Concinnity

Concinnity is the skillful and harmonious arrangement or fitting together of the different parts of something (Merriam Webster). Basically, it is several working parts existing in harmony, which is what our festival tries to accomplish. We bring people from all backgrounds together at MacLeish Field Station to enjoy art, food, music, and the outdoors. Come join us!

This year we will be holding our 2nd annual Concinnity Fest on April 27th (tentatively) at MacLeish Field Station! As always, the event will be free to all Five College students and friends.
